About the Library

DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP (DANBG Education), an ISO 9001 : 2015 certified organization, established in 2000, is today one of Indias most reputed and trusted Education Groups which houses engineering college, professional institutions and education centers across the country. Till date, DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP has transformed the lives of over 3 lakh students. Boosting a collection of around 8500 books spanning various disciplines, it serves as a cornerstone of academic exploration and intellectual growth. Equipped with two computers with internet facilities, the library embraces modern technology to complement its traditional print resources. Students have access to a wealth of online databases, e-books, and multimedia resources, enriching their research endeavours and expanding their horizons. Students gather for group discussions, study sessions, and workshops, enhancing their understanding of course materials and fostering a sense of camaraderie among peers.

Library Rules

  • Maintain silence at all times.
  • Return books within the due date.
  • No food or drinks allowed inside.
  • Handle books with care.
  • Respect other readers.
