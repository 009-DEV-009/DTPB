ABOUT US

DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP (DANBG Education), an ISO 9001 : 2015 certified organization, established in 2000, is today one of India’s most reputed and trusted Education Groups which houses engineering college, professional institutions and education centers across the country. Till date, DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP has transformed the lives of over 3 lakh students. Our commitment to high quality education over the last 23 years.

DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP is “West Bengal State Council of Technical & Vocational Education & Skill Development” (WBSCTVESD) affiliated organization from where we are conducting several numbers of Placement linked STVT courses from July-2012. Courses are:

  • Computer Application and Python Programming Language
  • Hardware and Networking, Food and Beverage Operations
  • Financial Accounting System
  • Medical Lab Technology
  • Physiotherapy Technician
  • Hospital and Health Management
  • ECG Technician
We are also affiliated under Paschim Banga Society for Skill Development under Govt. of West Bengal, from where we are conducting various types of skill development courses.

Additionally, we are working in 74 Government-sponsored schools on Computer Training Programs since 2004 in association with the School Education Department - Govt. of W.B.

DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP also conducts Information Technology, Management, General UG and PG Programs, in both Regular as well as Distance Mode. All our courses are approved by UGC and AICTE.

We are also associated with Phanshi Dewa Teachers Training Institute, Dist. Darjeeling, Block Phanshidewa, West Bengal, recognized by NCTE, where we conduct B.Ed and D.Ed programs.

Additionally, we offer various paramedical courses like Medical Lab Technology (MLT), Diploma in Medical Lab Technology (DMLT), and Bachelor of Medical Lab Technology (BMLT). All these courses are approved by AICTE, UGC, WBSCTVESD, and supported under the RNTU B-Voc program.

DATAPRO-AISECT N.B. GROUP also operates two English Medium Schools under affiliation with WBBSE.

Our Vision

Balurghat College, West Bengal, envisions a life-oriented education that empowers the students through a humanizing and liberating process, to be agents of transformation and development at different levels of life. Enabled and empowered, they respond proactively to concerns and conflicts inherent in today’s reality, especially those of women and persons who are unable to exercise their freedom to be human, and work for the integrity of creation.

Our Mission

  • To promote a value-based education.
  • To contribute to the transformation of society through social responsibility.
  • To promote inter-cultural and inter-religious harmony and concern for the human family.
  • To facilitate leadership among the students enabling them to proactively involve themselves in social liberation.
  • To provide quality, integral, vocational and career-oriented education.
  • To work towards the integrity of creation and foster interconnectedness, kinship, and eco-justice.
